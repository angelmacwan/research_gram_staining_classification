Our research addresses this gap by leveraging transfer learning–based methods on Gram-stained microscopy images to acheive accurate and precise classification of bacterial morphology and Gram characteristics. By utilizing pre-trained deep learning models, transfer learning substantially reduces the dependence on large, expertly annotated datasets, which remains a major challenge in microbiological imaging studies [i4–i6].
